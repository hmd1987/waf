Without a Face: Official Music Site

WAF has been slowly but surely creating new songs while keeping busy with other music projects such as an indie rock band named "Paper Gliders" and a heavy metal project named "Perfect Little Robot".

    Quick WAF History Lesson

  • Worst Debut Album Ever ranked #2 on Houston Chronicle Top Houston Discs of 2009 by music critic Joey Guerra
  • Honorable Mention (out of over 20,000 song submissions) in the 2012 International Songwriting Competition for the song Like A President
  • Winner in the rock category (out of over 9,000 song submissions) in the 2013 Unsigned Only Songwriting Competition for the song Like A President
  • Winner in the comedy/novelty category of the 2013 USA Songwriting Competition for the song Twisted Sexual Assault
    trigger warning: the song "Twisted Sexual Assault" was written using dark humor protesting the employment of convicted sex offenders at airports during a time when TSA was very new to the general public. It is written from the perspective of a predatory TSA agent.

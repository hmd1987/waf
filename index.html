Quick WAF History Lesson

  • Worst Debut Album Ever ranked #2 on Houston Chronicle Top Houston Discs of 2009 by music critic Joey Guerra
  • Honorable Mention out of over 20,000 submissions in the 2012 International Songwriting Competition for the song Like A President
  • Winner in the rock category of the 2013 Unsigned Only Songwriting Competition for the song Like A President
  • Winner in the comedy/novelty category of the 2013 USA Songwriting Competition for the song Twisted Sexual Assault
    trigger warning: the song "Twisted Sexual Assault" was written using dark humor
    protesting the employment of convicted sex offenders at airports
    during a time when TSA was very new to the general public.
    It is written from the perspective of a predatory TSA agent.

WAF released two full-length albums through Redbird Records & Touring:

  • Worst Debut Album Ever in 2009
  • The 1st Album Was Better in 2010
WAF parted ways with Redbird Records and went on to self-release:
  • 5 Songs (EP) in 2011
  • Oh, Great, An Election (EP) in 2012
  • The American Scheme (3rd full-length album) in 2013
  • SBNF (Single) in 2018

WAF has been slowly but surely creating new songs while keeping busy with other music projects
such as an indie rock band known as "Paper Gliders" and a heavy metal project named "Perfect Little Robot".
